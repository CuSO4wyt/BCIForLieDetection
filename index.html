Experiment Details:

1) Data Collection: Collect various types of biosignals including EEG, heart rate, blood oxygen levels, facial expressions (muscle activity analysis), and speech audio.

2) Participant Tasks: Participants are required to make statements under different scenarios (e.g., merit student evaluation, job interviews) to ensure data diversity and contextual complexity.

3) Data Processing: The collected raw data is standardized and processed to form a complete multimodal deception detection dataset, which serves as the foundation for subsequent model training.
