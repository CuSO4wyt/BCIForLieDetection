About Us 👋

We are a passionate research team from Nanjing University 🏫, exploring the frontier of brain-computer interface (BCI) technologies 🧠💡.

Our current project builds a multimodal deception detection system integrating EEG, heart rate, facial expressions, and speech 🧪🎙️ to connect neuroscience, AI, and human behavior 🌍🤖.

Thanks for visiting — stay tuned for updates, demos, and publications! 🚀📢

Experiment Demo 🎬

Acquisition and Construction of Multimodal Biosignals

(1) Collect EEG, heart rate, blood oxygen, facial expression (muscle activity), and speech data.

(2) Participants speak in diverse scenarios (e.g., merit evaluations, job interviews).

(3) Standardized data forms a multimodal deception detection dataset for model training.
