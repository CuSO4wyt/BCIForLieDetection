A Brain-Computer Interface-Based Deception Detection System

About Us 👋

We are a passionate research team from Nanjing University 🏫, driven by curiosity, creativity, and a shared mission to explore the frontier of brain-computer interface (BCI) technologies 🧠💡.

Our current project focuses on building a multimodal deception detection system that integrates physiological signals such as EEG, heart rate, facial expressions, and speech 🧪🎙️. We aim to bridge neuroscience, AI, and human behavior to create meaningful real-world applications 🌍🤖.

Thanks for visiting our page — stay tuned for updates, demos, and publications! 🚀📢
