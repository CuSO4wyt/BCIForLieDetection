About Us 👋

We are a passionate research team from Nanjing University 🏫, driven by curiosity, creativity, and a shared mission to explore the frontier of brain-computer interface (BCI) technologies 🧠💡.

Our current project focuses on building a multimodal deception detection system that integrates physiological signals such as EEG, heart rate, facial expressions, and speech 🧪🎙️. We aim to bridge neuroscience, AI, and human behavior to create meaningful real-world applications 🌍🤖.

Thanks for visiting our page — stay tuned for updates, demos, and publications! 🚀📢

Experiment Demo 🎬

Acquisition and Construction of Multimodal Biosignals

(1) Collect various types of biosignals including EEG (electroencephalogram), heart rate, blood oxygen levels, facial expressions (muscle activity analysis), and speech audio.

(2) Participants are required to make statements under different scenarios (e.g., merit student evaluation, job interviews) to ensure data diversity and contextual complexity.

(3) The collected raw data is standardized and processed to form a complete multimodal deception detection dataset, which serves as the foundation for subsequent model training.
